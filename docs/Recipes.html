Stir-fried and boiled tomato soup recipe

  1. Slice your tomatoes into thin circles.
  2. To a pan, add a heavy drizzle of pre-made tomato oil.
  3. Once they are lightly stir-fried, add a good amount of tomato juice, being careful not to completely fill the pan.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes, pour your tomatoes and your soup over rice, use toasted tomato seeds (and scrambled eggs, if you want) as pimpage, and enjoy!
